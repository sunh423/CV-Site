Hi! I am Howard --

a Product Manager in the making.

As a firm believer in the philosophy of life-long learning, I consider myself a highly driven individual and am currently in the process of expanding my skillset as a Product Manager.

With Product Management sitting in the center of business, tech and UX, I have all the skills needed to launch a product that your clients love.

Unlike many other traditional product managers that come from business or tech background, I graduated from University of Washington Seattle with a degree in Education, Communities in Organizations (ECO). I specialize in identifying and leveraging the key strengths of people and teams I work with, while centering on the organization's mission and values to guide my decisions.

Finally, I self-learn the technical skills needed to understand both the process behind idea implementation and metric data evaluation. Along with the extensive leadership skills gathered from working in diverse settings, I believe I have what it takes to move your company one step closer to its vision.
